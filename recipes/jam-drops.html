Jam Drops

Description

Jam drops for a blue day from Patacake. Super simple and not too sweet snacks. Easy to throw together for unexpected visitors.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup rolled oats (optional)
  • 120g melted butter or coconut oil
  • 1 tbs vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup raw sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients into a soft dough.
  2. Roll into ping pong size balls.
  3. Insert thumb into the middle of each ball to make well for the jam.
  4. Spoon in jam.
  5. Bake 15 mins at 180C.
  6. Eat and start to feel happier.
